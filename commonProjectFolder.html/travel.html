Do you want easy, quick way to enjoy sushi without having to do all of the work? Try to make our sushi bake, which takes 10 minutes to assemble and 20 minutes to bake in the oven. Our recipe feeds 8 people.

Ingredients: 1 avocado 1/2 cup soon sauce 2 cups of rice 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar 3 tablespoons of mayo 1 8 oz package of imitation crab 1 medium piece of salmon 1 cucumber 3 tablespoons of sriracha

Steps: Preheat oven to 350 degrees Make rice. Mix rice vinegar into rice after it is cooked Lay out all of the rice at the bottom of a large baking dish Lay out the imitation crab and cucumber on top of the rice Mix the imitation crab and 2 TBSP mayo into a crab salad Smear the crab salad onto the next layer Slice the avocado and lay those on the top of the dish Pour the rest of the mayo and sriracha on top Bake for 20 minutes
